# ADLAB 88 --- Software Brand Guide

## Brand Positioning

AdLab 88 is an experimental advertising system. It is structured,
analytical, and engineered.

This product is not positioned as a creative playground. It is a
controlled environment for experimentation and optimization.

Primary Identity: - Software-first - System-oriented - Editorial and
restrained - Confident, not flashy

Tagline: Run Better Experiments.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Core Visual Identity

### Color System

Primary Colors: - Warm Paper --- #F3ECDC - Deep Forest --- #1F3A32 -
Rust Accent --- #B55233

Neutral & Support: - Graphite Text --- #2A2A2A - Pure Black Outline ---
#111111

Color Rules: - Warm Paper is the dominant background. - Deep Forest is
reserved for structural elements (sidebar). - Rust is used only for
emphasis, action, and underlines. - No gradients. - No glow effects. -
No transparency overlays.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Typography System

### Headers

Monospace uppercase only. Recommended fonts: - IBM Plex Mono - JetBrains
Mono - Space Mono

Rules: - Uppercase for section titles. - Tight letter spacing. -
Moderate weight. - No exaggerated display fonts.

### Body

Clean sans-serif. Recommended: - Inter - Manrope

Rules: - Small uppercase for labels. - Sentence case for helper text. -
Data values may use monospace.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Layout Principles

-   Strict grid alignment.
-   Sharp rectangular edges only.
-   Thin black borders (1--2px).
-   Structured spacing.
-   No rounded corners.
-   No decorative floating shapes.
-   Subtle low-opacity square grid background.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## UI Tone & Voice

AdLab 88 speaks like a system, not a marketer.

Instead of: "Create powerful ads that convert instantly."

Use: "Generate structured ad variants."

Instead of: "Boost your growth now."

Use: "Optimize performance variables."

Tone Characteristics: - Direct - Analytical - Minimal adjectives -
Performance-focused

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Content Application

When used in social or content: - Use grid structures. - Maintain flat
color backgrounds. - Use strong hierarchy. - Use monospace headings. -
Avoid hype language.

AdLab 88 content should feel like: - Internal research notes - System
screenshots - Experiment logs - Data breakdowns

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## What To Avoid

-   Pastel-heavy trendy palettes
-   Memphis blobs and lightning shapes
-   Neon glow UI
-   Soft shadows
-   Rounded friendly SaaS UI kits
-   Marketing hype copy

AdLab 88 must always feel like a tool, not a toy.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Brand Philosophy

Build. Test. Optimize.

Run Better Experiments.
